Perpetually up in the Clouds

an abridged biography

she was born in the second month of the first year of the new millennium. she was an adored toddler and then a spunky child. one who enjoyed the girl scouts and webkinz. in middle school she took up ballet and graduated with a 4.0. and in high school, she élevéd to pointe, became a yearbook editor, and finished her four years with a 4.1 gpa.

now she is approaching her college graduation with a bs in psychology and a certificate in lgbt studies. she is on track to graduate from the barrett honors college with summa cum laude. her college career has been marked with extensive research experience, as both a research assistant and project coordinator for the autism brain and aging lab and the emerging minds lab respectively. she was an intern at the sexual orientation and gender institute of arizona. she has also nannied throughout college: abroad in spain, out of state in hawaii, and home in arizona. she is temporarily relocating to new york city after graduation with her girlfriend, amy, and their cat cornelia.

the rest is yet to come.
